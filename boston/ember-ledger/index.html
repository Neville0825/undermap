THE EMBER LEDGER

Starts in Harvard Square, Cambridge
3 chapters · 2 to 6 hours · pause anytime
Best for groups of 2–5 · ages 10+

On a storm-wrecked January night in 1764, Harvard's library burned to the ground. Five thousand books — a century of secrets — gone before dawn. That is what the histories say. The histories are wrong.

BEFORE YOU WALK

Make your way to Harvard Square — the plaza outside the T station entrance, by the news kiosk. When you arrive, the trail begins.

You'll need: comfortable shoes, a charged phone, and one earbud or a speaker for voice messages.

Chapter 1 is a two-hour walk. You can stop at the end of any chapter and return another day.
